FOLLOW THE STEPS GIVEN BELOW TO PLACE YOUR ORDER

  • Go online. Go to CoffeeHouse official website. At the middle of the website, there should be a button that says Click To Order.Click on that Button.
  • Decide what you want. From coffee, desserts and cakes, there is always something great to choose at CoffeeHouse! Just Select the thing that you want to order.
  • Now fill the required information of your order and provide your location and other credentials as shown in the picture.
  • Finish your order. Once you've finished making your order, click "Place Order".
